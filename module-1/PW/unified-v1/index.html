We are the Ideal Solution for the unification of ONGs in America

Working as a support for over 10 years, we at CTS have been able to revive many hopeless homes and environments and help them move to the next stage in their story. More than 3.200 houses were rebuilt in Cuba and Haiti, arund 500 educacional institutions were opended in the Brasilian northeast and an average of 200 scientific and technological laboratories and forms of shelter for needy children, who managed to find a new purpose in life.

How can we help!

With our system of integrating ONGS from different countries, we can help in different outdated parts of your region or that of an acquaintance! With the power of technology, the motto that two heads are better than one has become even stronger. Maybe you want to help the homeless, run a charity campaign or help your distant relative cross the border. In the face of all these difficulties, it is not always helping everyone or having enough resources together to supply children in a state of experiences and breaking barriers as a way to help everyone without excepton.

About us

Dedicated to helping the greatest number of people, with responsibility, quality and dexterity, we are a team that seeks for more than a decade to unite people, with willpower and focus, aiming to help others without receiving anything in return.
