Every day we imagine, study, and invent new ways to collaborate with people, and you can help us! If you want to become a contributor, go to the still contact page and fill out the formulary!

We are the Ideal Solution for the unification of ONGs in America

Working as a support for over 10 years, we at CTS have been able to revive many hopeless homes and environments and help them move to the next stage in their story. More than 3.200 houses were rebuilt in Cuba and Haiti, around 500 educacional institutions were opended in the Brasilian northeast and an average of 200 scientific and technological laboratories and forms of shelter for needy children, who managed to find a new purpose in life.

How can we help!

With our system of integrating ONGS from different countries, we can help in different outdated parts of your region or that of an acquaintance! With the power of technology, the motto that two heads are better than one has become even stronger. Maybe you want to help the homeless, run a charity campaign or help your distant relative cross the border. In the face of all these difficulties, it is not always helping everyone or having enough resources together to supply children in a state of experiences and breaking barriers as a way to help everyone without excepton.

With the impact of the pandemic, many doors have closed, however, this does not stop us from asking for help and uniting heads and hearts. Since 2020, we have brought, through our partners, ONGs from our partners, who were able to help and establish a link. On our success wall, you can check our contributions to society and you can also be part of the hands-on team! Whether you are a programmer who wants to help us connect even more people, an aspirant, or a person who wants to make a difference, you can sign up for free on our contact page, we will send you information about the availability of volunteer work within 42 hours on maximum, in the meantime, you can follow us on Instagram and Facebook and interact with the hands-on community!
